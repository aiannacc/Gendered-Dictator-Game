There are two lotteries on which you can base your earnings: The "Correct-Guess Lottery" or the "Gumball Lottery"

The "Correct-Guess" Lottery:

  • You win {{ Constants.GumballPrize }} tokens if your guess about the most recent dot grid was correct, but you win zero tokens if your guess was not correct.

The "Gumball" Lottery:

  • Imagine a gumball machine that has 100 gumballs in it.
  • Each gumball is either black or green.
  • After the experiment, one gumball will be drawn from this machine at random.
  • If that gumball turns out to be green, then you will win a prize of {{ Constants.GumballPrize }} tokens.
  • If the gumball is black, you win zero tokens.
