Instructions

You have been randomly and anonymously paired with another player. Each of you will be randomly assigned to the role of either the Agent or the Principal. You will learn your role before making any decision.

The Principal will choose whether or not to restrict the Agent's effort:

  • Restricted Agents may choose Effort = {2, 3, 4, 5}
  • Unrestricted Agents may choose Effort = {1, 2, 3, 4, 5}

The Agent will then choose the effort level, which determines the payoffs of both the Principal and the Agent:

Effort Level 1 2 3 4 5
Principal's Payoff 4 6 8 10 12
Agent's Payoff 13 12 11 10 9

We will randomly select a few (roughly 5) Agents and Principals for payment. We'll tell you how we convert lab dollars to real dollars later, but -- if you are randomly selected for payment -- you will get one extra real dollar for every lab dollar you earn.
