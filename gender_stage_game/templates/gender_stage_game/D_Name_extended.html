In this experiment, you have been assigned the role of {{ player.role }}. You will keep this role for the entire experiment.

You will be matched with a new Recipient every round. You will never be matched with the same Recipient more than once in the experiment.

In each round of the experiment, the Recipient is endowed with {{ Constants.endowment }}, from which the Decider can take any amount.

On the next screen, you will choose how much of your Round {{ player.round_number }} Receiver's {{Constants.endowment}} you will take, in increments of $0.50. Your Receiver will keep whatever is left.

While you choose how much to take, your Receiver will learn that your screenname is {{ group.name }}. They will then be asked to rate how socially appropriate it would be if {{ group.name }} took each potential amount between $0 and {{ Constants.endowment }}, in increments of $0.50.

Receivers earn a monetary prize if their rating of the appropriateness of the amount you decide to take turns out to be the most common rating given to that particular allocation by all other Receivers matched with Decider {{ group.name }}. You will see how your Round {{ player.round_number }} Receiver rated your chosen allocation at the end of the experiment, after you've finished making all of your allocation decisions.

After you have decided how much of the Receiver's {{ Constants.endowment }} to take, your Receiver will be shown your allocation decision and given the opportunity to write you a free-form message which you will view after you've finished making all of your allocation decisions.

All Deciders get a screenname in each round. Receivers do not have screennames.

In Round {{ player.round_number }}, your screenname is {{ group.name }}.
